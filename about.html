About Me

Hi! I'm Ben Raphael Piperno. I'm a Providence, RI based software engineer. I started my career studying Industrial Engineering at Northeastern University, and quickly started applying my degree towards quality systems engineering in the medical device and pharmaceutical space during my undergraduate internships (co-ops). After graduating with my Bachelor's of Science, I worked for 2 years as a consultant in the Biopharmaceutical industry. Since then, I've focused on expanding my impact and expertise in the Software as a Medical Device space to do my part for patients in need! Since then I've worked at companies like iRhythm (a leader in cardiac ambulatory monitoring) and Delsys Inc (a manufacturer of high fidelity Electromyography sensors) whlie completing my Master's in Computer Science back at my alma mater, Northeastern University.

Outside of work I love to cook (especially bake bread) and make art! I'm a massive fan of computer graphics and animation, and have taken several animation courses over the years. You can find me in the Boston and Providence area, or at home with my two cats, Tater and Tot.
